Dataset comes from Baimacheva et al. 2025 https://link.springer.com/article/10.1186/s13321-025-01080-7

The authors pulled data from CMRT (chiral molecular retention time) dataset, which contains a total of 11,720 enantiomeric pairs extracted from 644 articles. The authors focused on the largest subset of the data and after data cleaning and processing, the final dataset contains 1929 pairs of enantiomers, which have exactly one chiral center and contain the elements H, B, C, N, O, F, P, S, Cl, Br, or I.

The final dataset was downloaded from https://github.com/jairesdesousa/chiraldlsv/blob/main/TSNE_maps/class_all.csv
